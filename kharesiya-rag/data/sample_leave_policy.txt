# Kharesiya Leave Policy

## Overview
All full-time employees are entitled to paid leave as per company policy.

## Annual Leave
- Employees receive 18 days of annual leave per calendar year.
- Leave must be applied at least 3 working days in advance via the HR portal.
- Manager approval is required before leave is confirmed.

## Sick Leave
- Up to 10 sick days per year with medical certificate for absences over 2 consecutive days.
- Notify your manager before 10:00 AM on the day of absence.

## How to Apply for Leave
1. Log in to the HR portal at hr.kharesiya.internal
2. Navigate to Leave Management → Apply Leave
3. Select leave type (Annual / Sick / Casual)
4. Choose dates and add reason
5. Submit for manager approval
6. You will receive email confirmation once approved

## Casual Leave
- Maximum 5 casual days per year.
- Can be applied for same-day emergencies with manager notification.

## Contact
For questions, email hr@kharesiya.com or visit the HR desk on Floor 2.
